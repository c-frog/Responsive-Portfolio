About Me


Hello, my name is Clyde! I was born and raised in Boulder, CO, but have lived across the state. As a photographer I travel around a lot for work, and wouldn't have it any other way. I hope to add Web Developement to my resume so I can continue to travel and have a fulfilling personal life. I mainly spend my free time backcountry skiing, hiking 14er's, and racing a J24 sailboat.

In a few years I hope to move to Whitefish MT, and grow my wedding photography business. After visiting many ski towns, I have found Whitefish to be the most compelling, both for it's amazing mountain and surrounding lakes.
